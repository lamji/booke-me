## About Book.Me
Book.Me is a professional event booking management platform that helps clients plan and book premium events with ease. We specialize in managing personalized event experiences.

## Contact & Location
- **Email**: Available in our footer and from the settings panel (configured by admin)
- **Phone**: Displayed in the footer — contact us for the latest number
- **Address**: See the footer on the homepage for our current address
- **Working Hours**: By appointment — submit a booking and we'll confirm your slot

## Services & Event Packages
Event types and pricing are managed dynamically by the admin team. Use the booking form to see the current list of available packages. Examples may include:
- **Wedding Package** — Premium all-in-one wedding coordination
- **Birthday Package** — Complete birthday celebration management
- Add-ons such as live entertainment, décor, catering, etc. are selectable per event type

## Pricing
- All pricing is visible on the booking page per event type
- Base prices are set per package; add-ons carry individual prices (e.g., +₱1,500 for live band)
- Final price depends on the package chosen plus selected add-ons

## How Booking Works
1. Visit the Booking Page and select an Event Type
2. Choose your preferred date (must be a future date not already booked)
3. Select your preferred time slot
4. Fill in your personal details (Full Name, Email, Phone)
5. Optionally select add-ons
6. Submit — you will receive an email confirmation
7. Our team will review and approve or discuss adjustments

## Availability
- Dates are booked on a first-come, first-served basis
- Only one event per date is accepted (fully exclusive)
- To check availability for a specific date, simply ask Booky and I'll check for you in real time

## Booking Status Flow
- **Pending** → After you submit, your booking waits for admin approval
- **Approved** → Our team confirmed your slot. You'll receive an email
- **Cancelled** → The booking was declined or cancelled

## Finding Your Booking
- If you've already submitted a booking, you can look it up by visiting the "Find My Booking" feature using the email address you registered with

## Cancellation & Policies
- Cancellation policies are available in the footer under the "Policy" section
- For urgent inquiries, contact us via the provided email/phone
